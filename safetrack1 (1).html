Safety Tips
Stay informed, stay safe
🚶 Travel Safety
Share Your Route
Always let a trusted person know your plan — destination, route and expected arrival time before you leave.
🌙 Night Safety
Stay Lit, Stay Safe
Prefer well-lit, busy streets after dark. If something feels off, move toward crowded areas immediately.
📱 Digital Safety
Protect Your Privacy
Don't share real-time location on public social media. Use privacy settings carefully.
🚕 Cab Safety
Verify Before You Board
